AS AGRI AND AQUA LLP, established in March 2018, is India’s leading company that specializes in innovative and high-tech Agriculture and Aquaculture development and turnkey solutions.


Led by a group of 10 experienced and hardworking stalwarts from different verticals, A S AGRI AND AQUA LLP was formed to fulfil the vision that saw a revolutionized Agriculture and Aquaculture Ecosystem in India and ensured self-sufficiency, sustainability, and an improved socio-economic status for every Indian farmer.


Over the past few years, A S AGRI AND AQUA LLP has continued to cement its position as a prominent innovator, solution-provider, and a pioneering force in the Agriculture and Aquaculture sectors.


As a part of the A S Group of Companies conglomerate, A S AGRI AND AQUA LLP deals with research and development of cutting-edge technology and turnkey solutions in Soil-based Vertical Farming, Polyhouse construction, Aquaculture and Fish Farming, Health Care & Food Supplements, and more. We are also involved in projects like Turmeric Trading, Digital Rural Connectivity Projects, including our very own flagship e-Village app.


With the head office based in Thane, Maharashtra, A S AGRI AND AQUA’s reach spans across India, and continues to grow beyond the borders, including expansions in Oman, Qatar, UAE, UK, and Sri Lanka.
